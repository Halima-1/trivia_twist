TriviaTwist Quiz app

Game rules:
  • ⏱ You have 20 minutes to answer all 50 questions.
  • ✅ Once you select an answer, you cannot change it.
  • 🎯 Each correct answer gives you 1 point. No negative marking.
  • ⏩ Use the Next button to move to the next question.
  • 🛑 The quiz will end automatically when the timer runs out
  • 🏆 Your final score will be displayed at the end.
